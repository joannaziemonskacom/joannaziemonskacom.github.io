Hello!

My name is Joanna and I’m a freelance illustrator. I’m super lucky to live in the beautiful city of Barcelona, where inspirations are on every corner, no one ever sleeps, and you can always go out to get some sun.

My love to art was born a long time ago, when I was still a little kid and couldn’t even hold a pencil properly. Finally, after a lot of work and training, I had a chance to put my childhood dream into reality and become an artist.

Since my inner child has never grown up, I find a lot of ideas and inspirations in animated movies. I can endlessly read children stories, trying to imagine all the characters and their adventures. I love traveling, finding new places, being spontaneous, and playing with my two lovely cats, which I spend most of my work time with.

I would be happy to illustrate your book, story, game or whatever else you have in mind. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
