Hello!

My name is Joanna and I’m a freelance illustrator. I live in a small town in Poland with my husband, two cats, and a dog.

My love for art was born a long time ago when I was still a little kid and couldn’t even hold a pencil properly. Finally, after a lot of work and training, I had a chance to put my childhood dream into reality and become an artist.

Since my inner child has never grown up, I find a lot of ideas and inspirations in animated movies. I can endlessly read children's stories, trying to imagine all the characters and their adventures. I love traveling, finding new places, being spontaneous, and having long walks with my dog.

I would be happy to illustrate your book, story, game, or whatever else you have in mind. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
